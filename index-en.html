About me

Hello! My name is Maciej, and I have been creating modern websites, applications, and digital graphics for many years. I also produce a wide range of advertising print materials: banners, roll-ups, posters, leaflets, and more. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and CMS platforms like WordPress and Joomla. I build both simple business card websites and more advanced solutions tailored to individual needs. I also develop mobile applications and create 2D/3D graphics using both commercial and open-source software. Sharing knowledge is my passion – I’ve been actively involved in education for years, teaching others practical web design and programming skills.
